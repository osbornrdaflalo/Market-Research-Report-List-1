<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous</li><li>Homogenous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty inorganic catalysts market is primarily divided into two types: heterogeneous and homogeneous catalysts. Heterogeneous catalysts are solid-phase substances that facilitate reactions in a different phase, typically involving gases or liquids, enhancing efficiency and ease of separation. Homogeneous catalysts, in contrast, are present in the same phase as the reactants, often leading to more uniform reactions and high selectivity. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial processes, impacting efficiency, yield, and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
