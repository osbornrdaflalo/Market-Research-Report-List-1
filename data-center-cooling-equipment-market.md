<p><p>The data center cooling equipment market primarily comprises air cooling and water cooling equipment. Air cooling uses fans and air circulation systems to dissipate heat generated by servers, making it cost-effective and easy to implement. In contrast, water cooling employs chilled water or refrigerant systems to absorb and remove heat more efficiently, suitable for high-density environments. Each type offers distinct advantages based on factors like energy efficiency, scalability, and environmental considerations, catering to the diverse needs of modern data centers.</p></p>
