<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The General Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) market comprises three primary types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, and Vessel Sealing Units. Monopole ESUs use a single active electrode for cutting and coagulation, suitable for large tissue areas. Bipolar ESUs feature two electrodes, enabling precise control and minimal thermal spread, ideal for delicate tissues. Vessel Sealing Units are specialized for thermally sealing blood vessels, enhancing hemostasis and reducing operative bleeding. Together, these devices enhance surgical efficiency and safety.</p></p>
