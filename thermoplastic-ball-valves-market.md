<p><p>The thermoplastic ball valves market is segmented into four main types: union, flanged, threaded, and others. Flanged ball valves come with flanges for secure attachment to piping systems, ideal for high-pressure applications. Threaded ball valves use threaded connections for easy installation and removal, making them versatile.
