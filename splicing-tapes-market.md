<p><strong>The Splicing Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Repulpable</li><li>Non-Repulpable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The splicing tapes market is divided into two main types: repulpable and non-repulpable. Repulpable splicing tapes can be integrated back into the pulp process after use, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for paper and cardboard industries. In contrast, non-repulpable tapes are designed for stronger bonding and durability, often used in applications where tape remains permanently adhered. Each type serves distinct functions, catering to different industry needs based on their recyclability and adhesion properties.</p></p>
