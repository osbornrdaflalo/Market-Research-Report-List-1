<p><strong>The IoT RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li><li>Semi-Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID antennas market is categorized into three main types: Passive RFID, Active RFID, and Semi-Active RFID. Passive RFID relies on energy from a reader to function, making it cost-effective and maintenance-free, ideal for item tracking. Active RFID has its own power source, allowing for longer range and real-time data transmission, suitable for high-value asset tracking. Semi-Active RFID combines features of both, using a battery for extended range while responding to reader signals, enhancing versatility in various applications.</p></p>
