<p><p>The high pressure pipe fitting market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Straight Fitting**: These connect two pipes in a linear configuration.</p><p>2. **Elbow Fitting**: These create angles, typically 90 or 45 degrees, allowing for directional changes.</p><p>3. **Three-way Fitting**: Featuring three outlets, these distribute flow in multiple directions.</p><p>4. **Four-way Fitting**: This type has four ends, enabling connections between multiple pipes at various angles.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category encompasses specialized fittings for unique applications in high-pressure systems.</p></p>
