<p><p>The No Electricity Cooling Film market comprises primarily two types: Reflective and Radiant films. Reflective films operate by bouncing off sunlight and minimizing heat absorption, making surfaces cooler without power. Radiant films, on the other hand, function by emitting absorbed heat away from buildings or containers, enhancing cooling through passive thermal management. Both types are effective in reducing energy consumption, promoting sustainability, and maintaining desirable temperatures in various applications such as residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
