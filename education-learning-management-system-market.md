<p><p>The Education Learning Management System (LMS) market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based LMSs are hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy access, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for institutions looking for flexibility. Conversely, on-premises LMSs are installed locally on an institution's servers, providing greater control over data and customization, but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different institutional needs and technological capabilities.</p></p>
