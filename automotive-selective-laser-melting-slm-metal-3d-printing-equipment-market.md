<p><p>The Automotive Selective Laser Melting (SLM) Metal 3D Printing Equipment Market is categorized into two main types: "Single Laser" and "Multiple Laser" systems. Single Laser machines employ one laser to selectively melt metal powders layer by layer, making them suitable for lower production volumes and simpler designs. In contrast, Multiple Laser systems use several lasers simultaneously, significantly enhancing build speed and allowing for more complex geometries, making them ideal for high-volume production and intricate automotive parts.</p></p>
