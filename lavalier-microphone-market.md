<p><p>The lavalier microphone market is segmented into various types based on polar patterns. Omnidirectional microphones capture sound from all directions, making them ideal for interviews and presentations. Cardioid microphones focus on sound coming from the front, minimizing background noise, suitable for public speaking. Supercardioid microphones provide an even narrower pickup angle, offering greater isolation but increased sensitivity to rear sounds. Each type serves distinct applications in broadcasting, filmmaking, and public speaking, catering to diverse user needs in audio capture.</p></p>
