<p><p>The space simulator market encompasses various specialized types, each serving distinct functions in spacecraft design and operation. Sun sensor simulators replicate solar input data for attitude control systems, while ADCS (Attitude Determination and Control Systems) simulators model spacecraft orientation behavior. GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) simulators provide realistic satellite positioning and navigation data. Star tracker simulators emulate celestial navigation, crucial for precise orientation in space. Additional simulators include those for thermal, radiation, and communication systems, enhancing overall mission analysis and training.</p></p>
