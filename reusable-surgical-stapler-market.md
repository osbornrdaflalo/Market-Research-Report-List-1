<p><p>The reusable surgical stapler market is primarily divided into two types: manual surgical staplers and powered surgical staplers. Manual surgical staplers are operated by hand, requiring the surgeon to apply pressure to activate the staple closure, often providing greater control during procedures. In contrast, powered surgical staplers use mechanical or battery-operated systems, offering consistent staple formation and efficiency, particularly in complex surgeries. Both types are designed for durability and reusability, catering to varying surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
