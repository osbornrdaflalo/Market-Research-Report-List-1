<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Test Handler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li><li>Strip Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor chip test handler market features several types, each designed for specific testing requirements. Gravity handlers utilize gravity for the movement of chips, ensuring efficient handling. Turret handlers rotate to access multiple test positions, optimizing space and speed. Pick-and-place handlers use robotic arms to accurately position chips, enhancing precision in testing. Finally, strip handlers are designed for handling batches or strips of chips simultaneously, offering high throughput for mass production environments. Each type caters to varying operational needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
