<p><strong>The Micrographic Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Micrograph</li><li>Electron Micrograph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micrographic camera market is primarily divided into two types: optical micrographs and electron micrographs. Optical micrographs use visible light and optical lenses to capture detailed images of specimens, making them ideal for biological and material sciences applications. In contrast, electron micrographs utilize electron beams to create high-resolution images of samples at a microscopic level, revealing structures not visible under optical microscopes. Both types serve essential roles in research, quality control, and diagnostics across various industries.</p></p>
