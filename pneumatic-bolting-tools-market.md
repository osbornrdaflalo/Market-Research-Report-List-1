<p><strong>The Pneumatic Bolting Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tensioning Tool</li><li>Torque Tool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic bolting tools market is primarily divided into two main types: tensioning tools and torque tools. Tensioning tools apply a precise amount of force to stretch bolts, ensuring uniform load distribution and preventing loosening over time, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Torque tools, on the other hand, control the rotational force applied to fasteners to achieve desired tightness, focusing on accuracy and efficiency. Both tools are essential for industries requiring reliable and safe bolting solutions, enhancing productivity and reducing maintenance.</p></p>
