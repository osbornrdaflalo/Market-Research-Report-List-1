<p><strong>The Medical-Grade Silicone Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Consistency Rubber (HCR)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade silicone rubber market comprises two main types: High Consistency Rubber (HCR) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HCR is known for its robust physical properties and is typically processed in a solid form, making it suitable for products like prosthetics and tubing. In contrast, LSR is a low-viscosity liquid that cures into a flexible material, ideal for intricate designs and applications such as seals, gaskets, and medical device components. Both types ensure biocompatibility and reliability in medical settings.</p></p>
