In contrast, double-sided conductive fiberglass tape has adhesive on both sides, providing superior bonding for complex surfaces and enhanced thermal management. Both types are crucial in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive, offering durability, conductivity, and versatility for various insulation and shielding applications.</p></p>
