<p><p>The dental intraoral X-ray market encompasses various types, including bitewing X-rays, periapical X-rays, and others. Bitewing X-rays are used to detect cavities between teeth and assess the bone level. Periapical X-rays capture the entire tooth, from crown to root, aiding in identifying issues such as abscesses or bone loss. Other types may include occlusal X-rays, panoramic views, and cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT). Each type serves specific diagnostic purposes, enhancing dental care and treatment planning.</p></p>
