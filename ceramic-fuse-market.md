<p><p>The ceramic fuse market is categorized into three main types: high voltage, low voltage, and safety voltage ceramic fuses. High voltage ceramic fuses are designed for applications requiring protection in power systems with voltages typically above 1 kV, ensuring safety in industrial settings. Low voltage ceramic fuses, used in residential and commercial applications, protect circuits in environments that operate under 1 kV. Safety voltage ceramic fuses are specifically built for sensitive electronic devices, providing reliable protection at lower voltage levels to prevent damage.</p></p>
