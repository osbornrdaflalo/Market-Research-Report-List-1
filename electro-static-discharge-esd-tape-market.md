<p><p>The Electro-Static Discharge (ESD) tape market encompasses two main types: single-sided and double-sided tape. Single-sided ESD tape features a conductive adhesive on one side, ideal for grounding static-sensitive devices or surfaces. Double-sided ESD tape, with conductive layers on both sides, is used for bonding and securing components while minimizing static discharge. Both types are crucial in electronics manufacturing and assembly, providing protection against static buildup and ensuring the integrity of sensitive electronic components during handling and transport.</p></p>
