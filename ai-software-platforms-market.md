<p><p>AI software platforms can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. They facilitate real-time data processing and access from anywhere, making them ideal for businesses seeking agility. On-premises solutions, conversely, involve installing software on local servers, providing enhanced security and control over data.
