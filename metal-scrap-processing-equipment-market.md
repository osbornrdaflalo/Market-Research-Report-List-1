<p><p>The metal scrap processing equipment market comprises various types including baler presses, shredders, granulators, shears, separators, and others. Baler presses compact scrap into bales for easy handling and transport. Shredders reduce metal size for further processing, while granulators produce smaller particles, ideal for recycling. Shears cut large metal pieces into manageable sizes. Separators sort metals from contaminants, enhancing recycling efficiency. Other equipment may include conveyors and magnetic separators, facilitating streamlined operations in metal recycling facilities.</p></p>
