<p><strong>The Aircraft Carbon Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drum Brakes</li><li>Disk Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft carbon brakes market primarily features two types: drum brakes and disc brakes. Drum brakes utilize a cylindrical drum housing that rotates with the wheel, using friction against shoes to slow down the aircraft. In contrast, disc brakes consist of a flat rotor and caliper system, applying pressure via brake pads for more efficient heat dissipation and performance. Disc brakes are increasingly preferred for their superior stopping power and lighter weight, which are crucial for modern aviation demands.</p></p>
