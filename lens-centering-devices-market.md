<p><ul><li>Mechanical Alignment Technique</li><li>Active Optical Assembly Technique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lens centering devices market is primarily categorized into two main types: mechanical alignment techniques and active optical assembly techniques. Mechanical alignment techniques involve physical adjustment methods to ensure precise lens positioning, often using fixtures or gauges for alignment. In contrast, active optical assembly techniques utilize advanced optical systems and sensors to dynamically adjust lens positioning in real-time, enhancing accuracy and efficiency. Both methods cater to various applications in industries such as optics, aerospace, and telecommunications, ensuring optimal performance of optical systems.</p></p>
