<p><p>The augmented and mixed reality market includes various display technologies tailored for immersive experiences. Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) are wearable devices that completely enclose the user's field of vision, offering fully immersive virtual environments. In contrast, Head Up Displays (HUDs) project information onto a transparent visor or screen, allowing users to see both digital content and their physical surroundings simultaneously. These technologies cater to different applications, from gaming and training to industrial and automotive sectors, enhancing user interaction and engagement.</p></p>
