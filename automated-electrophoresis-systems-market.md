<p><p>Automated electrophoresis systems are categorized into fully automated and semi-automated types based on their operational capabilities. Fully automated systems perform the entire electrophoresis process, including sample loading, analysis, and data interpretation, with minimal human intervention. This enhances throughput and consistency. In contrast, semi-automated systems require some manual steps, such as sample preparation or loading, while still providing automated analysis. These systems offer a balance between convenience and user control, catering to diverse laboratory needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
