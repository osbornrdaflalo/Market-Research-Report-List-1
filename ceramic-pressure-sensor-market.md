<p><p>The ceramic pressure sensor market offers two primary output signal types: simulation output signals and digital output signals. Simulation output signals provide a continuous variable voltage or current proportional to the measured pressure, allowing for straightforward analog integration into existing systems. In contrast, digital output signals convert pressure measurements into digital data, facilitating enhanced precision, reduced susceptibility to noise, and ease of interfacing with modern digital systems. This differentiation supports varied application needs across industries, improving measurement accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
