<p><p>The non-woven fabrics/textiles market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Spunmelt** - produced by combining spunbond and melt-blown processes, offering high strength and filtration properties.</p><p>2. **Wet Laid** - made by dispersing fibers in water, resulting in a strong, uniform fabric, often used in medical and filtration applications.</p><p>3. **Dry Laid** - created by carding fibers and bonding them through mechanical or thermal methods, suitable for various consumer products.</p><p>4. **Others** - encompasses specialized techniques like needle-punched and stitch-bonded fabrics, catering to niche markets and applications.</p></p>
