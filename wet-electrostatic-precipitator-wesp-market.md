<p><p>Wet Electrostatic Precipitators (WESPs) are advanced air pollution control devices designed to remove particulate matter from gas streams using electrostatic forces in a wet environment. The cross-flow type WESP features a horizontal flow of gas, allowing for efficient particle collection while minimizing space requirements. In contrast, the vertical-flow type WESP allows exhaust gases to pass vertically through the electrodes, optimizing collection efficiency and providing effective moisture management. Both types are essential for industries aiming to meet stringent emission regulations and enhance environmental performance.</p></p>
