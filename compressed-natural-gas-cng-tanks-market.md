<p><p>The Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) tanks market comprises three primary types: metal, glass fiber, and carbon fiber tanks. Metal CNG tanks are durable and cost-effective but heavier. Glass fiber tanks, combining fiberglass and resin, offer a lightweight option with good resistance to corrosion. Carbon fiber tanks are the lightest and strongest, providing higher storage density and efficiency but at a premium cost. Each type serves specific applications based on weight, strength, and economic considerations, supporting the growing demand for CNG vehicles.</p></p>
