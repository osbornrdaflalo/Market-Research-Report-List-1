<p><p>The AC-to-DC adapters market is segmented primarily into center positive (+) and center negative (-) connect types, which refer to the polarity of the output connector. Center positive adapters have the positive voltage at the center pin, commonly used in devices like laptops and gaming consoles, ensuring they receive correct power supply. In contrast, center negative adapters feature the negative voltage at the center pin, frequently used in equipment like audio devices and some handheld electronics. Each type caters to specific power requirements and device compatibility.</p></p>
