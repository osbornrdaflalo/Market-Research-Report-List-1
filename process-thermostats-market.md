<p><p>The process thermostats market is primarily divided into line-voltage and low-voltage thermostats. Line-voltage thermostats operate directly with high voltage electrical systems, controlling devices like electric heaters and providing quick temperature regulation. In contrast, low-voltage thermostats are designed for systems such as HVACs, utilizing a lower voltage to manage boilers, air conditioners, and other climate control systems. Each type offers distinct advantages in energy efficiency and application suitability, catering to different consumer needs in temperature management solutions.</p></p>
