<p><strong>The Protein Biochip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Chip</li><li>Passive Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Protein Biochip market is categorized into two main types: active chips and passive chips. Active chips employ integrated microelectronic components to enhance functionality, allowing for real-time analysis and higher throughput in protein interactions. They often feature on-board processing capabilities. In contrast, passive chips rely on immobilized proteins and do not incorporate electronic components, limiting their functionality but offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for various applications such as diagnostics and research. Both types play crucial roles in biomarker discovery and disease diagnosis.</p></p>
