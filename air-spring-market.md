<p><p>The air spring market includes three main types: Convoluted Bellows, Rolling Lobe Bellows, and Sleeve Bellows. Convoluted Bellows feature a multi-lobed design, offering high load capacity and flexibility, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Rolling Lobe Bellows have a single, smooth lobe, providing consistent performance and durability, commonly used in automotive and industrial systems. Sleeve Bellows are cylindrical with a simpler design, offering lightweight and compact solutions for lighter loads and space-constrained applications. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, durability, and application requirements.</p></p>
