<p><p>The metal zipper market comprises three main types: close-end, open-end, and two-way zippers. Close-end zippers are designed for one-directional use, typically found in garments and bags. Open-end zippers can fully separate, making them ideal for applications like jackets and luggage. Two-way zippers feature two sliders, allowing for versatile opening and closing from both ends, commonly used in larger items such as tents and sleeping bags. Each type serves distinct functions across various industries.</p></p>
