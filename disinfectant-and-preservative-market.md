<p><p>The disinfectant and preservative market includes various types: **Alcohols & Aldehydes** are effective against microbes, with alcohols like ethanol and aldehydes like formaldehyde widely used. **Oxidizing Agents**, such as hydrogen peroxide, kill pathogens by oxidation. **Biguanides, Amides & Quaternary Ammonium Compounds** disrupt cell membranes, ensuring broad-spectrum antimicrobial action. **Phenol & Derivatives** denature proteins and disrupt cell walls. **Silver and Iodine Compounds** release ions that inhibit microbial growth. **Others** include niche or emerging agents with unique mechanisms, catering to diverse disinfection and preservation needs.</p></p>
