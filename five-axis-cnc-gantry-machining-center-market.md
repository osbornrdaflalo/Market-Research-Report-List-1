<p><strong>The Five-Axis CNC Gantry Machining Center Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Beam Type</li><li>Moving Beam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Five-Axis CNC Gantry Machining Center market includes two primary types: Fixed Beam Type and Moving Beam Type. The Fixed Beam Type features a stationary beam structure with the workpiece moving beneath it, ideal for large, heavy components requiring precise machining. In contrast, the Moving Beam Type has the beam that travels along the gantry, providing flexibility in tool positioning and accommodating varied workpiece sizes. Both types effectively enhance machining efficiency, precision, and versatility in complex manufacturing applications.</p></p>
