<p><p>The silicone-based pressure sensitive adhesives (PSAs) market is divided into two main types: peroxide cure and platinum cure adhesives. Peroxide cure PSAs are typically used for applications requiring high temperature resistance and durability, as they form strong bonds when cross-linked with peroxides. In contrast, platinum cure PSAs offer superior clarity, flexibility, and low volatility, making them ideal for sensitive applications such as electronic devices and medical products. Both types provide strong adhesion and are essential in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
