<p><p>Glasses-free 3D displays encompass various technologies that enable three-dimensional viewing without the need for special eyewear. Light Barrier Technology uses a grid of barriers to direct light into each eye. Lenticular Lens Technology employs an array of lenses that refract light, creating depth perception. Directional Backlight utilizes specific lighting arrangements to produce 3D effects. Direct Imaging projects 3D images directly onto a screen. Other methods may include holography or volumetric displays, enhancing user experience across various applications.</p></p>
