<p><p>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Equipment market encompasses various technologies, notably Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes powerful lasers to fuse metal powders layer by layer, producing high-density parts with intricate geometries. In contrast, EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum, allowing for precise melting of metal powder, often yielding superior mechanical properties. Other methods include Binder Jetting and Direct Energy Deposition, broadening the market's capabilities for diverse applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
