<p><p>Electroless nickel plating services are categorized based on phosphorus content. Medium-phosphorus electroless nickel (6-10% phosphorus) offers a balance of corrosion resistance and hardness, suitable for various industrial applications. Low-phosphorus electroless nickel (less than 6% phosphorus) provides excellent wear resistance and is often used in electronics. High-phosphorus electroless nickel (more than 10% phosphorus) delivers superior corrosion resistance and is ideal for harsh environments. Each type serves distinct industries, catering to diverse performance requirements and applications.</p></p>
