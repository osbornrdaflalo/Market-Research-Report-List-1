<p><p>The Human Lyophilized Rabies Vaccine market is categorized into two main types: Pre-Exposure Vaccines and Post-Exposure Vaccines. Pre-Exposure Vaccines are administered to individuals at higher risk of rabies exposure, such as veterinarians or travelers to endemic regions, to build immunity before contact. Conversely, Post-Exposure Vaccines are provided after potential exposure to the virus, typically following animal bites, to prevent the onset of rabies. Both types are crucial for managing rabies risk and ensuring public health safety.</p></p>
