<p><p>The water leakage detector systems market is divided into two main types: positioning and non-positioning systems. Positioning water leakage detector systems are designed to identify the exact location of a leak, using advanced sensors and technology for precise detection. In contrast, non-positioning systems provide alerts when a leak is detected but do not specify its location. Both types are crucial for preventing water damage, with positioning systems offering enhanced operational efficiency and targeted maintenance capabilities, while non-positioning systems deliver broader coverage and simplicity.</p></p>
