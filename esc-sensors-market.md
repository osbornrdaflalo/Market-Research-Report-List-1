<p><p>The ESC (Electronic Stability Control) sensors market includes several key components that enhance vehicle stability and safety. Steering angle sensors determine the direction of steering input, essential for effective vehicle maneuvering. Yaw rate sensors capture the vehicle's rotational speed around its vertical axis, while lateral acceleration sensors measure side-to-side acceleration. Together, these sensors provide critical data for ESC systems to maintain vehicle stability and prevent skidding.</p></p>
