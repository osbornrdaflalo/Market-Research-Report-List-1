<p><ul><li>Single-V Beveled End</li><li>Double-V Beveled End</li><li>I-Type Butt Joint</li><li>Single-U Beveled End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe beveling service market offers various types of bevels to enhance weld integrity and fitting. Single-V beveled ends involve a single angled groove for efficient welding. Double-V beveled ends feature two grooves, allowing deeper penetration for stronger joints. I-Type butt joints create a flat surface for seamless alignment during welding. Single-U beveled ends provide a U-shaped groove, promoting better fusion. Each type caters to specific welding requirements, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in pipe construction and maintenance.</p></p>
