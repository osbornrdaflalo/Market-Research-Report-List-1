<p><strong>The Laboratory Leak Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hammering Method</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Leak Tester market primarily consists of two types: the Hammering Method and Ultrasound. The Hammering Method involves creating pressure changes by striking a sample, identifying leaks through sound or disruption in airflow. In contrast, the Ultrasound method utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect leaks, pinpointing issues by analyzing acoustic emissions from escaping gases or liquids. Both techniques serve critical roles in ensuring the integrity of laboratory equipment and systems, enhancing safety and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
