<p><p>The internal combustion engine market is primarily divided into two categories: two-stroke and four-stroke engines. Two-stroke engines complete a power cycle in two strokes of the piston, offering higher power-to-weight ratios and simplicity, making them ideal for smaller applications like motorcycles and chainsaws. In contrast, four-stroke engines complete a cycle in four strokes, emphasizing fuel efficiency and lower emissions, suitable for vehicles and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance, efficiency, and application.</p></p>
