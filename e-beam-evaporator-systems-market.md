<p><ul><li>Multi-pocket e-beam Sources</li><li>Single Pocket e-beam Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-beam evaporator systems encompass two primary market types: multi-pocket and single-pocket e-beam sources. Multi-pocket e-beam sources feature multiple independent pockets, allowing simultaneous evaporation of different materials, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in coating applications. In contrast, single-pocket e-beam sources utilize a singular chamber for material deposition, making them simpler and more cost-effective for specific applications. Both systems are integral to precision vacuum coating processes in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, optics, and advanced materials.</p></p>
