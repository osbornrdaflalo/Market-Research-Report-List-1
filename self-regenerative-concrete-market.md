<p><ul><li>Capsule Self Regenerative Concrete</li><li>Intrinsic Self Regenerative Concrete</li><li>Vascular Self Regenerative Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-regenerative concrete market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. Capsule self-regenerative concrete involves microcapsules containing healing agents that release upon crack formation, promoting self-healing. </p><p>2. Intrinsic self-regenerative concrete incorporates materials within the concrete matrix that enable automatic healing when exposed to moisture, fostering durability. </p><p>3. Vascular self-regenerative concrete features a vascular network, allowing nutrients or healing agents to flow and repair cracks, enhancing structural longevity. Each type offers innovative solutions for extending the life of concrete structures.</p></p>
