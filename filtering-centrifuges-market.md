<p><p>The filtering centrifuges market is segmented into two primary types: horizontal and vertical centrifuges. Horizontal centrifuges are designed with a horizontal orientation, facilitating continuous operation and efficient solid-liquid separation, commonly used in applications like wastewater treatment and chemical processing. Vertical centrifuges, on the other hand, feature a vertical configuration, offering advantages in batch processes and higher sedimentation rates. Both types serve critical roles across industries like pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and oil refining, tailoring to specific separation needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
