<p><p>The electronic packaging silica gel desiccants market comprises three primary types: white, blue, and orange silica gel. White silica gel is non-indicating and absorbs moisture silently, making it widely used for electronics to prevent damage. Blue silica gel contains cobalt chloride, changing color from blue to pink when saturated, providing a visual cue for replacement. Orange silica gel, often made with a non-toxic indicator, shifts from orange to green, combining aesthetic appeal with functionality in moisture control for sensitive electronic components.</p></p>
