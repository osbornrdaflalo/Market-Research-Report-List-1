<p><p>Air freight services encompass various market types, notably On-Board Courier (OBC) and Daily Flight markets. On-Board Courier involves a courier accompanying the shipment on a commercial flight, ensuring expedited delivery and personal handling of high-value or time-sensitive items. In contrast, the Daily Flight market offers regular scheduled air freight services, accommodating a range of cargo volumes with fixed routes and frequencies. Both options cater to specific logistics needs, balancing urgency, cost, and shipment complexity efficiently.</p></p>
