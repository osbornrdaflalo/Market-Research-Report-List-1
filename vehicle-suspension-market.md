<p><p>The vehicle suspension market comprises various types, notably hydraulically actuated suspension and electronically actuated suspension. Hydraulically actuated suspension systems utilize fluid pressure to adjust the damping and ride height, offering a balance between comfort and handling. In contrast, electronically actuated suspension employs sensors and electronic controls to dynamically adjust the suspension settings in real-time, enhancing performance and ride quality. Both types aim to improve vehicle stability and passenger comfort, catering to the diverse needs of modern automotive designs.</p></p>
