<p><ul><li>Oil Impregnated Paper (OIP)</li><li>Resin Impregnated Paper (RIP)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical bushings market comprises several types, including Oil Impregnated Paper (OIP) and Resin Impregnated Paper (RIP). OIP bushings utilize oil-soaked paper insulation, offering excellent dielectric properties and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. In contrast, RIP bushings use synthetic resin for insulation, providing enhanced mechanical strength and moisture resistance, ideal for various environmental conditions. Additionally, "Others" encompasses alternative materials and technologies, catering to specialized applications and evolving market demands.</p></p>
