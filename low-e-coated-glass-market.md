<p><strong>The Low-E Coated Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Online Pyrolytic Deposition</li><li>Offline Vacuum Sputtering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low-E coated glass market is characterized by two primary types of coatings: online pyrolytic deposition and offline vacuum sputtering. Online pyrolytic deposition involves applying a thin layer of low-emissivity coating during the glass manufacturing process, resulting in cost-effectiveness and high durability. In contrast, offline vacuum sputtering occurs after glass production, allowing for precise control over coating thickness and properties, enhancing energy efficiency. Both methods significantly improve thermal insulation and energy conservation in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
