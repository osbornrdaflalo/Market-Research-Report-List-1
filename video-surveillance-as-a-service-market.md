<p><p>Video Surveillance as a Service (VSaaS) encompasses three primary market types: Hosted, Managed, and Hybrid. Hosted VSaaS involves cloud-based solutions where video data is stored and managed remotely by a service provider. Managed VSaaS offers additional support services, including system maintenance and monitoring, often on-premises or through a combination of local and cloud systems. Hybrid VSaaS combines both hosted and managed services, allowing businesses flexibility in data storage and management while leveraging the benefits of both approaches for enhanced security.</p></p>
