<p><p>The fat-reduced cocoa powder market is primarily segmented into two types: natural and alkalized cocoa powder. Natural cocoa powder is derived from roasted cocoa beans without any chemical treatments, retaining a light brown color and a more acidic flavor profile, commonly used in baking and beverages. In contrast, alkalized cocoa powder undergoes a Dutch processing method, which neutralizes acidity and results in a darker color with a smoother taste, making it ideal for chocolates and desserts. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences in the food industry.</p></p>
