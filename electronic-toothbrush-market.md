<p><ul><li>Vibration Motion</li><li>Rotation-Oscillation Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic toothbrush market features two main types based on their motion mechanisms: vibration motion and rotation-oscillation motion. Vibration motion brushes use high-frequency vibrations to effectively dislodge plaque and stimulate gums, offering a gentle yet thorough cleaning experience. In contrast, rotation-oscillation brushes employ a circular motion that rotates and oscillates to efficiently remove debris and plaque. Both types enhance oral hygiene, catering to diverse consumer preferences and providing effective alternatives to traditional manual brushing methods.</p></p>
