<p><strong>The Harbour Crane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mobile</li><li>Fixed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harbour crane markets are categorized into mobile and fixed types based on their operational characteristics. Mobile harbour cranes are versatile and can be relocated easily within the port, enabling efficient handling of cargo across different areas. They are ideal for temporary projects or varied unloading operations. In contrast, fixed harbour cranes are stationary installations designed for specific loading and unloading tasks. These typically provide greater lifting capacity and stability but lack the flexibility of mobile units, serving designated locations consistently.</p></p>
