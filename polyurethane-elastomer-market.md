<p><strong>The Polyurethane Elastomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset PU Elastomers</li><li>Thermoplastic PU Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane elastomer market is primarily divided into two types: thermoset PU elastomers and thermoplastic PU elastomers. Thermoset PU elastomers are characterized by their rigid structure after curing, offering excellent chemical and heat resistance, making them suitable for applications like coatings and gaskets. In contrast, thermoplastic PU elastomers are versatile, combining the properties of rubber and plastic, allowing for easy processing and recycling. These are widely used in flexible applications such as automotive interiors and sports equipment.</p></p>
