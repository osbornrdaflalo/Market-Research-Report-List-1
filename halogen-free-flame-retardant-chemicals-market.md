<p><p>The Halogen-Free Flame Retardant Chemicals market includes various types, notably Aluminum Hydroxide and Organo-Phosphorus Chemicals. Aluminum Hydroxide acts as a non-toxic flame retardant by releasing water vapor when heated, thereby cooling the material and inhibiting combustion. Organo-Phosphorus Chemicals, on the other hand, function by forming a protective char layer when exposed to heat, which slows down the spread of flames. Both types are crucial in promoting fire safety in diverse applications, such as electronics, construction, and textiles.</p></p>
