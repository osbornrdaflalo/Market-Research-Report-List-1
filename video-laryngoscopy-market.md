<p><strong>The Video Laryngoscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channelled Video Laryngoscopy</li><li>Unchannelled Video Laryngoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video laryngoscopy market consists of two primary types: channelled and unchannelled video laryngoscopy. Channelled video laryngoscopes feature a guiding channel that allows for the introduction of instruments, such as endotracheal tubes, enhancing the accuracy of intubation in challenging airway situations. In contrast, unchannelled video laryngoscopes provide a direct view without a guiding channel, emphasizing visualization and straightforward intubation techniques. Both types play critical roles in modern anesthesia and emergency medicine, improving patient safety and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
