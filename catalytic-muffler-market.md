<p><p>The catalytic muffler market includes three main types: Box Type, U Type, and In-line Catalytic Mufflers. Box Type Catalytic Mufflers are compact and designed for space efficiency, commonly used in various vehicle applications. U Type Catalytic Mufflers feature a U-shaped design that enhances exhaust flow and noise reduction. In-line Catalytic Mufflers are installed directly in the exhaust system for optimal catalytic conversion and minimal backpressure. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific vehicle performance and emission control needs.</p></p>
