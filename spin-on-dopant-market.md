<p><strong>The Spin-on Dopant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spin-on Dopant market consists of two primary types: P-type and N-type dopants. P-type dopants introduce elements such as boron to create 'holes' in the semiconductor lattice, increasing positive charge carriers. Conversely, N-type dopants, like phosphorus or arsenic, add extra electrons, enhancing negative charge carriers. Both types are critical in semiconductor fabrication, enabling precise control over electrical properties. This segmentation caters to diverse applications in electronics, photovoltaics, and advanced materials development, driving innovation in device performance and efficiency.</p></p>
