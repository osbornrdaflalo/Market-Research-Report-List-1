<p><p>The Photovoltaic Connector market comprises primarily two types: AC and DC connectors. AC Photovoltaic Connectors are used to connect alternating current outputs from solar inverters to the electrical grid, facilitating seamless energy transfer. In contrast, DC Photovoltaic Connectors are designed for direct current applications, linking solar panels to inverters and optimizing energy flow in solar power systems. Both connector types ensure safe, efficient, and reliable connections within solar energy installations, supporting the growing demand for renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
