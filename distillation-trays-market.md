<p><ul><li>Bubble Cap Trays</li><li>Sieve Trays</li><li>Valve Trays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distillation trays market primarily comprises bubble cap trays, sieve trays, and valve trays, each designed for efficient mass transfer and separation in distillation processes. Bubble cap trays feature caps that allow vapor to pass through liquid, enhancing contact. Sieve trays have perforated surfaces facilitating vapor flow, offering simplicity and low pressure drop. Valve trays incorporate movable valves that optimize liquid and vapor interaction, providing flexibility in operation. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, influencing separation efficiency and operational costs.</p></p>
