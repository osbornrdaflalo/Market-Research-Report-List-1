<p><strong>The LNG Liquefaction Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coiled Heat Exchanger</li><li>Plate - fin Heat Exchanger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNG liquefaction heat exchanger market comprises two main types: coiled heat exchangers and plate-fin heat exchangers. Coiled heat exchangers feature a spiral design that promotes efficient heat transfer in compact spaces, making them ideal for smaller LNG facilities. In contrast, plate-fin heat exchangers utilize flat plates with fins to maximize surface area, enhancing heat exchange efficiency. Both types serve crucial roles in the LNG liquefaction process, contributing to energy efficiency and operational effectiveness in gas processing facilities.</p></p>
