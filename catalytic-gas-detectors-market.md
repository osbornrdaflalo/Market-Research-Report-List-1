<p><ul><li>Point Detector</li><li>Open Path Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The catalytic gas detectors market includes two main types: point detectors and open path detectors. Point detectors are designed to monitor and detect gas concentrations at specific locations, providing targeted safety measures for confined spaces. In contrast, open path detectors survey a wider area by creating an invisible beam between sensors, identifying gas leaks along the path and offering early detection over large spaces. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in industrial environments by quickly identifying hazardous gases.</p></p>
