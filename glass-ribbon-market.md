<p><p>The glass ribbon market encompasses various types, including single-layer glass and laminated glass. Single-layer glass is a straightforward, unmodified product used in applications like windows and displays due to its clarity and simplicity. In contrast, laminated glass consists of two or more glass layers bonded with an interlayer, providing enhanced safety, security, and sound insulation. These types serve distinct purposes across industries, with laminated glass being preferred in environments requiring additional durability and protection.</p></p>
