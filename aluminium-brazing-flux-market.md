<p><p>The aluminum brazing flux market is primarily categorized into two types: powder and paste. Powder flux is often used for its ease of application and uniform distribution, making it ideal for various industrial processes. It is typically applied in powdered form, allowing for precise control during brazing. In contrast, paste flux offers better adhesion and is easier to manage in specific applications, providing a thicker layer of protection against oxidation. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of aluminum joining processes.</p></p>
