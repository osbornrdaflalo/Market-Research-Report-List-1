<p><strong>The Life Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Term Insurance</li><li>Permanent Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Life insurance is primarily categorized into two markets: term insurance and permanent insurance. Term insurance provides coverage for a specified period, offering a death benefit if the insured passes away during that term. It is typically more affordable but does not build cash value. In contrast, permanent insurance offers lifetime coverage, accumulating cash value over time, which can be borrowed against or withdrawn. While more expensive, it provides long-term financial security and an investment component.</p></p>
