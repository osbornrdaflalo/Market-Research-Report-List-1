<p><p>The Automotive Dual Variable Valve Timing (VVT) market includes various types such as Late Intake Valve Closing (LIVC), Early Intake Valve Closing (EIVC), Early Intake Valve Opening (EIVO), and others. LIVC delays intake valve closure to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. EIVC closes the intake valve early to enhance low-speed torque and efficiency. EIVO opens the intake valve sooner for better high-speed performance. Other types focus on optimizing valve timing for specific engine conditions, balancing power, efficiency, and emissions.</p></p>
