<p><strong>The Wireless Gaming Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>M Wave</li><li>Decimeter Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless gaming headsets market can be categorized into three main types based on their transmission technology: Infrared, M Wave, and Decimeter Wave. Infrared headsets utilize light waves for short-range audio transmission, often requiring a direct line of sight. M Wave headsets operate in the microwave frequency range, providing a balance between range and sound quality, ideal for gaming. Decimeter Wave headsets, operating in the UHF band, offer greater penetration and range, making them suitable for immersive gaming experiences without interference.</p></p>
